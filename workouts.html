Basic Workout Routines

Upper Body Workouts

Push-ups

  • Sets: 3
  • Reps: 10-15
  • Rest: 60 seconds

Targets chest, shoulders, and triceps

Dumbbell Rows

  • Sets: 3
  • Reps: 12 each side
  • Rest: 60 seconds

Targets back and biceps

Lower Body Workouts

Bodyweight Squats

  • Sets: 4
  • Reps: 15-20
  • Rest: 90 seconds

Targets quadriceps, hamstrings, and glutes

Walking Lunges

  • Sets: 3
  • Reps: 12 each leg
  • Rest: 60 seconds

Targets legs and improves balance

Core Workouts

Plank Hold

  • Sets: 3
  • Duration: 30-60 seconds
  • Rest: 45 seconds

Strengthens core and improves stability

Bicycle Crunches

  • Sets: 3
  • Reps: 20 total
  • Rest: 45 seconds

Targets abs and obliques
